About Us
About SRISHTI KARMA FOUNDATION
SRISHTI KARMA FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization dedicated to working with underprivileged children, delivering sports programs. SKF utilizes athletics to enhance children’s health, improve academic performance, and reduce crime. Founded in 2022 by Mr. Harcharan Singh, who discovered his potential through sports, SKF aims to harness the power of sports for the betterment of more youngsters. The foundation provides high-quality sports coaching to empower youth, creating an environment conducive to achieving sporting excellence with the right spirit.
OUR MISSION
Our mission is to make opportunities and infrastructure accessible to all sports enthusiasts, fostering excellence in their chosen discipline. We aim to develop and harness talent, enabling individuals to become outstanding through sports
OUR OBJECCTIVES
  • Run sports academies/clubs.
  • Provide advanced coaching for excellence at all levels (State, National & International).
  • Deliver top-notch training from experienced coaches, instilling sportsmanship values.
  • Identify and nurture talent at the grassroots level.
  • Offer high-quality support and resources to the weaker sections of society.
  • Provide health and nutritional guidelines.
  • Promote and accelerate support development in India.
  • Advocate for physical activity as an integral part of daily life.
  • Organize as a public welfare society, working for the welfare of the public in various ways.
